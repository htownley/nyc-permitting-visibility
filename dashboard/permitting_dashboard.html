Source: NYC Open Data (Socrata) via the open-source civic-ai-tools MCP server; rebuild with python3 build_dashboard.py. DOB NOW figures cover initial filings ("-I1") only. All time series cohort by decision date; quarterly grain throughout; current partial quarter excluded. "Days to approval" is calendar days and cannot separate applicant-held from agency-held time. The clock chart carries a second (right) axis for volume — muted bars confined to the bottom band (the price–volume idiom), context not a correlated measure. Segments: new house = New Building ≤3 units; small apt 4–25 (25 = CA AB 2234's statutory threshold); large apt 26–99; tower = 100+ units or 10+ stories; homeowner reno = 1/2/3-family alterations; the rest = business & other work.
